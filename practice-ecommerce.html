Practice tasks

9 tasks — try them, then reveal the answer

Build each one yourself in Excel (or Power BI) first. The answer shows a working Excel formula, the result from this dataset, and how you'd do it in Power BI.

Task 1. What are total sales?

Show answer
=SUM(H2:H251)

Answer: $316,302

In Power BI: Card: SUM(Sales).

Task 2. What is total profit?

Show answer
=SUM(K2:K251)

Answer: $12,984

In Power BI: Card: SUM(Profit).

Task 3. What is the overall profit margin (profit ÷ sales)?

Show answer
=SUM(K2:K251)/SUM(H2:H251)

Answer: 4.1%

In Power BI: Measure: DIVIDE(SUM(Profit), SUM(Sales)), formatted as %.

Task 4. What are total sales in the Technology category?

Show answer
=SUMIF(F:F,"Technology",H:H)

Answer: $150,461

In Power BI: SUM(Sales) filtered to Category = Technology.

Task 5. How many orders are loss-making (Profit < 0)?

Show answer
=COUNTIF(K:K,"<0")

Answer: 98

In Power BI: Card: COUNTROWS(FILTER(Orders, Orders[Profit]<0)).

Task 6. Which category is the most profitable?

Show answer
PivotTable: Category → Rows, Sum of Profit → Values (or SUMIF per category)

Answer: Furniture ($6,185)

In Power BI: Bar chart of SUM(Profit) by Category.

Task 7. What is the average discount given?

Show answer
=AVERAGE(J2:J251)

Answer: 9.9%

In Power BI: Card: AVERAGE(Discount) as %.

Task 8. What are total sales in Germany?

Show answer
=SUMIF(E:E,"Germany",H:H)

Answer: $61,392

In Power BI: SUM(Sales) filtered to Country = Germany; or a Map visual.

Task 9. What are total sales for the Consumer segment in Technology?

Show answer
=SUMIFS(H:H,D:D,"Consumer",F:F,"Technology")

Answer: $45,738

In Power BI: SUM(Sales) with two filters: Segment = Consumer, Category = Technology.
