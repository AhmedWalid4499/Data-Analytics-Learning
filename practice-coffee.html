Practice tasks

8 tasks — try them, then reveal the answer

Build each one yourself in Excel (or Power BI) first. The answer shows a working Excel formula, the result from this dataset, and how you'd do it in Power BI.

Task 1. What is the total revenue across all transactions?

Show answer
=SUM(H2:H221)

Answer: $1,255.70

In Power BI: A Card visual showing SUM(Total).

Task 2. What is the average transaction value?

Show answer
=AVERAGE(H2:H221)

Answer: $5.71

In Power BI: A Card with AVERAGE(Total).

Task 3. How many Lattes were sold (total quantity)?

Show answer
=SUMIF(D:D,"Latte",F:F)

Answer: 28

In Power BI: Card: CALCULATE(SUM(Quantity), Product = "Latte").

Task 4. How many transactions were over $20?

Show answer
=COUNTIF(H:H,">20")

Answer: 0

In Power BI: Card with a measure: COUNTROWS(FILTER(Sales, Sales[Total]>20)).

Task 5. What is the total revenue from the Airport store?

Show answer
=SUMIF(C:C,"Airport",H:H)

Answer: $344.90

In Power BI: Filter a SUM(Total) measure to Store = Airport.

Task 6. Which store has the highest total revenue?

Show answer
=SUMIFS(H:H,C:C,"Downtown") … compare per store (or use a PivotTable: Store → Rows, Sum of Total → Values)

Answer: Mall ($350.10)

In Power BI: Bar chart of SUM(Total) by Store, sorted descending.

Task 7. Which product generates the most revenue?

Show answer
PivotTable: Product → Rows, Sum of Total → Values, sort descending (or SUMIF per product)

Answer: Sandwich ($143.00)

In Power BI: Bar chart of SUM(Total) by Product, Top N = 1.

Task 8. What percentage of transactions were paid by Card?

Show answer
=COUNTIF(I:I,"Card")/COUNTA(I2:I221)

Answer: 58.6%

In Power BI: Card visual: DIVIDE(COUNTROWS(FILTER(Sales,Payment="Card")), COUNTROWS(Sales)).
