Practice tasks

8 tasks — try them, then reveal the answer

Build each one yourself in Excel (or Power BI) first. The answer shows a working Excel formula, the result from this dataset, and how you'd do it in Power BI.

Task 1. What is the highest box office figure ($M)?

Show answer
=MAX(F2:F181)

Answer: $1,160.5M

In Power BI: Card: MAX(Box Office).

Task 2. What is the average IMDb rating?

Show answer
=AVERAGE(G2:G181)

Answer: 6.74

In Power BI: Card: AVERAGE(IMDb Rating).

Task 3. How many Action movies are there?

Show answer
=COUNTIF(B:B,"Action")

Answer: 27

In Power BI: Card filtered to Genre = Action.

Task 4. What is the average budget of Sci-Fi movies ($M)?

Show answer
=AVERAGEIF(B:B,"Sci-Fi",E:E)

Answer: $131.3M

In Power BI: AVERAGE(Budget) filtered to Genre = Sci-Fi.

Task 5. How many movies are rated above 8.0?

Show answer
=COUNTIF(G:G,">8")

Answer: 29

In Power BI: Card: COUNTROWS(FILTER(Movies, Rating>8)).

Task 6. What is the total box office for 2019 ($M)?

Show answer
=SUMIF(C:C,2019,F:F)

Answer: $2,643.5M

In Power BI: SUM(Box Office) filtered to Year = 2019.

Task 7. Which genre has the highest average rating?

Show answer
PivotTable: Genre → Rows, Average of IMDb Rating → Values

Answer: Action (7.10)

In Power BI: Bar chart of AVERAGE(Rating) by Genre.

Task 8. Which director has the highest total box office?

Show answer
PivotTable: Director → Rows, Sum of Box Office → Values, sort descending

Answer: C. Vance ($8,227.1M)

In Power BI: Bar chart of SUM(Box Office) by Director, Top N = 1.
