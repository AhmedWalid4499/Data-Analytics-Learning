Worth holding

Certifications, honestly assessed

Start here

PL-900 · Power Platform Fundamentals

The friendliest entry point. No prerequisites, no coding, and it covers Power Apps, Power Automate and Power BI at a conceptual level. Fundamentals certifications don't expire, so it's a permanent line on your CV. The exam runs to a tight 45-minute limit, so practise pacing.

Official page →

For BI-heavy roles

PL-300 · Power BI Data Analyst Associate

The serious Power BI credential, covering preparing, modeling, visualizing and securing data. The exam costs $165 USD (varying slightly by region), with a passing score of 700 out of 1000. The credential is valid for one year and can be renewed free through an online assessment on Microsoft Learn. Expect scenario-based questions — hands-on practice matters more than memorising.

Official page → · Study guide →

!
Heads-up on the Power Automate cert (PL-500). The PL-500 exam and the Power Automate RPA Developer Associate certification retire on June 30, 2026, with no direct replacement announced. If you're learning Power Automate now, skip chasing this credential — get the skills via the free Learn paths instead, and let PL-900 cover the certification side. Microsoft is broadly shifting toward AI-focused certifications, so watch the Microsoft Learn site for what replaces it.

Certification details change — always confirm current cost, format and validity on the official Microsoft Learn page before booking.
