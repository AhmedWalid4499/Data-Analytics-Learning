Practice tasks

9 tasks — try them, then reveal the answer

Build each one yourself in Excel (or Power BI) first. The answer shows a working Excel formula, the result from this dataset, and how you'd do it in Power BI.

Task 1. What is the total headcount in the file?

Show answer
=COUNTA(A2:A151)

Answer: 150

In Power BI: Card: COUNTROWS(Employees).

Task 2. How many employees are currently Active?

Show answer
=COUNTIF(J:J,"Active")

Answer: 132

In Power BI: Card filtered to Status = Active.

Task 3. What is the average salary across everyone?

Show answer
=AVERAGE(H2:H151)

Answer: $69,290

In Power BI: Card: AVERAGE(Salary).

Task 4. What is the total payroll of the Engineering department?

Show answer
=SUMIF(C:C,"Engineering",H:H)

Answer: $2,223,500

In Power BI: SUM(Salary) filtered to Department = Engineering.

Task 5. What is the average salary in Sales?

Show answer
=AVERAGEIF(C:C,"Sales",H:H)

Answer: $65,712

In Power BI: AVERAGE(Salary) with Department = Sales.

Task 6. What is the highest salary in the company?

Show answer
=MAX(H2:H151)

Answer: $116,500

In Power BI: Card: MAX(Salary).

Task 7. Which department has the highest average salary?

Show answer
PivotTable: Department → Rows, Average of Salary → Values (or AVERAGEIF per dept)

Answer: Engineering ($96,674)

In Power BI: Bar chart of AVERAGE(Salary) by Department.

Task 8. How many employees have Left (attrition count)?

Show answer
=COUNTIF(J:J,"Left")

Answer: 18 (12.0% of staff)

In Power BI: Card filtered to Status = Left; show as % with DIVIDE.

Task 9. What is the average performance rating?

Show answer
=AVERAGE(I2:I151)

Answer: 3.58

In Power BI: Card: AVERAGE(Rating).
